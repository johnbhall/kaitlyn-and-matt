Getting to Washington, D.C.

There are 3 airports that serve the Washington, D.C. metro area: Reagan National Airport (DCA) is the closest (~15 minutes) while Baltimore-Washington International (BWI) and Dulles International (IAD) are 30-45 minutes from downtown Washington. Amtrak trains arrive at Union Station.
